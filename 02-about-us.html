About Us

McGrew Behavior Services

MBIS has been offering Training, Consultation, Behavioral Assessment, Planning and Intervention, Mental Health Therapy, and 1 to 1 Behavior Assistance for people with Autism and other developmental disabilites since 2001.

We believe in more than just learning objectives, but treatment should also consider the individual’s activities and rituals of daily life.

Direction

Support

MBIS Staff are supervised by a licensed clinician and Board Certified Behavior Analyst

How We Succeed.

  • Observe

    We begin our process by observing the individual in their environment to identify the behaviors which need immediate care.

  • Strategize

    Our Board Certified Behavior Analysts review the gathered information and build a customized plan to ensure change.

  • Engage

    Our team of Board Certified Behavior Analysts and Assistant Behavior Analystis implement the strategy to stimulate behavior change.

  • Review

    We constantly monitor, review, and re-strategize the patient’s behavior plan to increase their ability to succeed.

Participting in this program, consumers and their support teams will acquire tools and skills that will assist them in managing behavior challenges. The development of these skills is designed to support individuals in the least restrictive environment, to enable individuals to participate more fully in their communities, to promote satisfying life and interpersonal relationships, encourage independent functioning, and to lay the groundwork for future productivity.

- Deborah McGrew BCBA-D, MFT, LPCC
Executive Director and Founder, MBIS Inc.
